METRICS

TOXICITY

The Toxicity Scanner provides a mechanism to analyze and mitigate the toxicity of text content, playing a crucial role in maintaining the health and safety of online interactions.

ANONYMIZE

The Anonymize Scanner acts as your digital guardian, ensuring your user prompts remain confidential and free from sensitive data exposure.

INVISIBLE TEXT

The Invisible Text Scanner is designed to detect and remove non-printable, invisible Unicode characters from text inputs.

PROMPT INJECTION

It is specifically tailored to guard against crafty input manipulations targeting large language models (LLM).
